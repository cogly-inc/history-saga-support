History Saga

Privacy Policy

Overview

History Saga does not require an account, does not collect personal information, and does not sell or share any data with third parties.

Data We Collect

We collect anonymous usage analytics to help us improve the app. This includes:

  • Which screens and features are used
  • Session duration
  • App performance metrics

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you. We use PostHog to process this data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No names, emails, or personal information
  • No location data
  • No contacts or photos
  • No user accounts or login credentials

Data Storage

Your progress in the app is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to it.

Third-Party Services

We use PostHog for anonymous analytics. You can review their privacy policy at posthog.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version on this page.
