About the App

History Saga brings history to life through interactive stories of the world's most fascinating figures. Complete character stories to unlock new ones and journey through time.

FAQ

  • How do I unlock new characters? Complete the current character's story to unlock the next one in the sequence.
  • How do I resume a chapter? Your progress is saved automatically. Just tap the character and select the chapter to pick up where you left off.
  • The app isn't playing audio. Make sure your device isn't on silent mode and the volume is turned up.

Requirements

iOS 17.0 or later. Works on iPhone and iPad.
